About TK

While Tae Kyung was enrolled in Savannah College of Art and Design for two years, TK has developed a strong interest in both digital and film photography. He has further pursued his passion by becoming a photographer intern in SAS. He is currently serving his National Service in Korea in the Recon unit and hopes to return back to the photography industry by April 2020. He hopes his portfolio will encourage people to feel the best moment in every occasion.
